Cipher α — Caesar Shift

The Romans used this on military dispatches. Each letter shifts a fixed number of places back through the alphabet. Try shift of 3.

▸ Decryption help

A Caesar shift of 3 means A→D, B→E, C→F... to decode, shift backwards: P→M, D→A, Q→N. The letters look unfamiliar — but they spell something you know.

Cipher β — Anagram
